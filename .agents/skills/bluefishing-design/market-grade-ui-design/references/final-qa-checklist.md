# Final QA Checklist

Before delivering:

- Spanish Chile copy, tú-form, no emoji.
- Cobalt only for human primary action.
- Teal only for agent/AI/automation.
- Red only for urgent/danger.
- All critical data has source/evidence.
- Every AI decision shows confidence, rule or reasoning.
- Tables have sticky headers and stable columns.
- Buttons name the exact action.
- Loading, empty, error, pending, review, success and archived states exist when relevant.
- Keyboard and focus states are visible.
- Mobile does not overlap text, tables or actions.
- Motion is short, purposeful and reduced-motion safe.
- No purple gradients, decorative blobs, nested cards or fake marketing copy inside the app.
